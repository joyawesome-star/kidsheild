Safeguarding Commutes with Smart Tags

A High-Margin, Hardware-Enabled SaaS Business Model Redefining Student Security across India.

The Vulnerability Window

Over 120 Million children commute daily in India. The transit window of dispersal, boarding, and pool-car handoffs is highly chaotic. Existing solutions like expensive tracking watches are banned in schools, while paper IDs tear easily, leaving kids vulnerable without reliable dynamic contact links in emergencies.

How It Works

  • Scan the Tag: A teacher, driver, or bystander scans the high-contrast QR code during transit confusion or emergency
  • Instant Access: A responsive, secure web profile loads in under 3 seconds without installing any application
  • One-Click Call: The scanner views verified guardian coordinates, driver routes, or medical flags to immediately resolve tracking stress

Premium Physical Hardware: A highly durable, lightweight, 3mm thick premium acrylic keychain cut into an attractive kid-friendly backpack shape. Built fully weatherproof to survive rough handling and torrential monsoons, hanging securely on schoolbags indefinitely.

Dynamic Cloud Dashboard: When scanned via smartphone, it instantly launches a lightweight, app-less mobile landing profile displaying verified contact routes. Parents update phone details in real-time via secure OTP, protecting sensitive home address fields from public exposure.
